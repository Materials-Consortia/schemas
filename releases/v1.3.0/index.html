For example, a 10 ps simulation with calculation steps of 1 fs where data is stored once every 50 fs, nframes will be 200.

            The indices of a set of frames that give a good but very brief overview of the trajectory. The first reference frame could for example be a starting configuration, the second a transition state and the third the final state.

            A list of species items. A species item is a list describing the species of the sites of this structure.
